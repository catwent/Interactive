Website as

My website is an open field full of rice paper screens.


I can create spatial subdivisons with ease, or "rooms" within rooms.


I can leave clear pathways from one "room" to the next, or make things more hidden.


The field, being a large expanse, allows the possibility for me to create as many more rooms as I wish.


The rooms are open-air; breeze flows in and out from other parts of the world, and my rooms are inherently interconnected with it.


The rooms are open to the public; anyone is welcome to walk through.


I can take anything I want from the world and place it in my open-air rooms, or I could spend time to cultivate something from the ground.
